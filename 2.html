FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is BalanceSplitter free to use? ⌄

Yes! Our core features are free forever. Premium plans unlock advanced analytics, unlimited groups and priority support.

Can I use it for international trips? ⌄

Absolutely. BalanceSplitter supports 150+ currencies with automatic live conversion rates.

Is my financial data secure? ⌄

We use bank-grade AES-256 encryption, optional 2FA, and never sell your data. Ever.

Can I export my expense history? ⌄

Yes, export any group or personal history to CSV or PDF anytime from Settlement History.

Does it work on mobile? ⌄

BalanceSplitter is fully responsive and installs as a PWA on iOS & Android home screens.
